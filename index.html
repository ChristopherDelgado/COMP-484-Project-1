"Opinions"

IGN gives Deathloop a score of 10/10. The game tells it's story with the overused audio logs and files however as IGN states they are concise, deeply flavourful, and often surprisingly funny. Although audio logs and files in most games are used to tell some backstory of the world to the player, in Deathloop they play very detrimental roles not finding some audio log or file can prevent the player from finding clues to help them kill their targets, they also provide backstory on the 8 Visionaries which you are out to kill. IGN can attest to the latter, they write:

“Directly tying objectives to these diaries and messages means each new discovery further develops an authentic antagonistic relationship between you and the Visionaries as you play, rather than having a manufactured one dropped in your lap.”

My Opinion

I personally think that DeathLoop is an amazing game! First it really takes the whole learn from your mistakes thing and runs with it well. And while you might think that basically playing the same levels over and over might get boring, trust me when I say it doesn't multiple times I have found myself going back to the same areas with new trinkets and finding new paths accessible or new weapons that I couldn't reach before. For example SPOILER WARNING: when following one of the Visionary Leads there is a section that requires you to turn off some beer taps but while doing this you have to cross a hallway full of mines and tripwires while also avoiding getting seen by nearby enemies. Initially I found this incredibly difficult and could not get it done - as you have to reset if you fail - but after finding the right combination of slabs and trinkets I was able to pull it off. I took with me the Aether slab with the ghost and flicker upgrades as well as the Mine Own character trinket. With this specific loadout I was finally able to finish that lead, and I think that this truly points out what DeathLoop is all about. Killing, Looting, Looping, with different loadouts for different results.
